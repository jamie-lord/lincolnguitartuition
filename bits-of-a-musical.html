Composition

Bits of a musical.

These are three bits of a musical I wrote with my friend James Colclough, a great theatre producer and singing teacher (one of his students currently starring in Mamma Mia in London's West End). I wrote the songs and most of the plot — and Jim said "no, you can't do that, or that — but how about this?" And he was bleedin' right every time. Great working with you, Jim.
