Composition

Bits of a musical.

These are 3 bits of a musical which I wrote with my friend James Colclough, a great theatre producer and singing teacher. (One of his students currently starring in Mamma Mia in London's West End.) I wrote the songs and most of the plot, and Jim said "No, you can't do that, or that — but how about this?" And he was bleedin' right every time. Great working with you, Jim!
