DAN McCAUGHERN · MA COMPOSING (KINGSTON, LONDON)

"His accessible approach and totally inspiring music writing has led us to commissioning Dan a number of times."

NIKKI-KATE HEYES MBE · CEO, SOUNDLINCS

Dan McCaughern has been playing professionally for about 32 years, throughout the UK, Ireland and Europe — on television, in theatre and on radio. As a composer, his works have been performed at The O2, in London's West End and by a 112-piece orchestra in Lincoln Cathedral, as well as various other venues throughout Europe.

He is now permanently based in Lincoln, teaching, composing and rehearsing with his band, Fallen House. His home studio was the first Rock School exam centre in Lincolnshire, and he uses the studio facilities during teaching to let students experience recording-studio work.
