{% include section-header.html number="02" label="About" title="32 years on stage, in studios and in classrooms." %}
DAN McCAUGHERN · MA COMPOSING (KINGSTON, LONDON)

"His accessible approach and totally inspiring music writing has led us to commissioning Dan a number of times."

NIKKI-KATE HEYES MBE · CEO, SOUNDLINCS

I've been playing professionally for around 32 years — across the UK, Ireland and Europe — on TV, in theatre, and on radio. As a composer, my works have been performed at The O2, in London's West End, and by a 112-piece orchestra in Lincoln Cathedral.

I'm now permanently based in Lincoln, teaching, composing and rehearsing with my band, Fallen House. My home studio was the first Rock School exam centre in Lincolnshire, and students get to use the studio facilities during lessons.
